Comparison of the election programs of the parties for the 2021 german federal election.

INTRODUCTION

The content of the webpage is developed as a student project for the course "Visualisation". The goal is to get quick insights about the content of the election programs of the parties for the 2021 german federal election.

OPTIONS

In the following, various options can be used that can also change the results. Since the calculations are very complex, loading times can be long.

At the beginning only the election programs of the parties currently represented in the Bundestag are considered. If you want to see all parties that are eligible for election in the constituency (Erfurt - Weimar - Weimarer Land II (constituency 193)), please tick the box.
At the beginning only the frequency of the words within an election program is calculated. (Term Frequency) With the selection of Inverse Document Frequency (IDF), the occurrence of a word in all election programs is taken into account. Words that only appear in a few election programs are considered more important than words that are represented in all documents.
Stemming or the normal form reduction should help to convert the words to their normal form. Since there can be many changes to a root word in the German language, this function was implemented. Unfortunately, this only works to a limited extent here. For the sake of interest, however, this function was retained and made available as an option.

COMPARISON CLOUD

In this wordcloud you can choose two parties whose most commonly used words are presented with the respective colors. The more important the party's word, the larger and more colorful it is.

Bar chart

The bar chart is used to get an overview about keywords for all chosen parties. It is possible to just look single keywords up. There is also the possibility to change the ordering.
